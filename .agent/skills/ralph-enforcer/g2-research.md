# Gate 2: Technical Research

**Context**: Don't reinvent the wheel.
**Goal**: Validate the approach with external best practices.

## Steps
1.  **Search**: Use `search_web` to find libraries, patterns, or similar implementations.
2.  **Document**: Create `tech-research-<TASK_ID>.log`.
3.  **Content**: List 3 URLs and summaries of findings.
4.  **Verify**: Ensure the proposed solution aligns with industry standards.
